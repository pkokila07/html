WELCOME KIM SEOK JIN

MOON

Kim Seokjin, widely known as Jin, is a South Korean Singer, Songwriter, Producer, and Actor.
He is a member of the globally acclaimed boy band BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan).
Born on December 4, 1992, in Gwacheon, South Korea,
Jin is celebrated for his versatile talents,
captivating stage presence, and charming personality.
