Tender coconut water is generally considered a safe and healthy beverage,
and it's often recommended for hydration due to
its natural electrolyte content. The glycemic index (GI) of
coconut water is generally considered high people with diabetes
should be cautious about their tender coconut intake. The sugar content
of tender coconut water can vary depending on factors such as the maturity
of the coconut and the season in which it is harvested.
There are several algorithms that can be used for size estimating of tender coconut.
One commonly used approach is based on image processing techniques,
such as edge detection, thresholding, and contour analysis. The project aim
is to develop an automated system for detecting
and classifying different types of tender coconuts based
on their external characteristics using a
combination of the Laplacian of Gaussian (LoG)
edge detection algorithm
